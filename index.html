BEGINNER BLUEPRINT

A no-fluff 4-day training program built for anyone starting from zero. Follow it for 8–12 weeks, stay consistent, and you will see results. Guaranteed.

Consistency First
4 days a week, every week. Missing sessions is the number one reason beginners don't see results.
02
Progressive Overload
Add weight or reps every 1–2 weeks. If it doesn't challenge you, it doesn't change you.
03
Form Over Ego
Light weight with perfect form builds more muscle than heavy weight with bad form. Always.
04
Rest & Recover
Muscle is built outside the gym. Sleep 6–8 hours. Rest days are part of the program.

Nutrition Basics

2g
per kg of bodyweight, every day. Chicken, beef, eggs, fish, Greek yogurt.

+200

Big T's Rules to Live By

🔥
Warm Up Every Session
5–10 min on the bike or treadmill + 2 warm-up sets per first exercise. Cold muscles get injured.
📈
Track Your Lifts
Write down every weight and rep. If you can't see progress, you can't chase it.
😴
Sleep Is the Secret Weapon
Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep. 6–8 hours is the real anabolic window.
⚡
Don't Skip the Boring Stuff
Face pulls, calf raises, planks — the unsexy exercises protect your joints long-term.
